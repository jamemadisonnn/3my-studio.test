Azme Rahmat

Born in 1995, Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Worked in a Malaysian niche video marketing advertising company, MTAS SDN BHD (M.T.A.S. 2017)

Azme is a multi-talented creative with a passion for visual arts and a keen eye for capturing moments through photography. With a skill set that encompasses photography, photo and video editing, as well as social media design.

As a photographer, Azme has honed their ability to create stunning images that evoke emotion and tell stories.

In addition to photography, Azme possesses a mastery of photo and video editing techniques. They skillfully transform raw footage and images into polished works of art, enhancing colors, adjusting lighting, and adding visual effects to bring out the true essence of each piece.

Beyond the realm of digital arts, Azme also enjoys exploring their creativity through simple art latte. With a playful touch, they can turn a regular cup of coffee into a delightful piece of temporary artwork, capturing the essence of their fun-loving spirit.

Azme is constantly seeking new ways to express their creativity and is committed to delivering high-quality visual content that exceeds client expectations. Whether it's capturing special moments, refining visuals through editing, or adding a touch of whimsy to a cup of coffee, Azme is dedicated to producing work that leaves a lasting impression.
